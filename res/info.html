Keybinds

Function Key
Toggle playback (Press) SPACE
Switch screenshot mode (Press) T
Toggle axis (Press) W
Toggle axis labels (Press) E
Toggle bounding box (Press) Q
Lock movement to horizontal axis (Hold) Z
Lock movement to vertical axis (Hold) X
Decrease mouse sensitivity (Hold) C
Camera default view (Press) V
Camera isometric view (Press) B
Unlock sliders* (Press) L

*Changing scale and generation multipler sliders may cause unpredictable behavior.
